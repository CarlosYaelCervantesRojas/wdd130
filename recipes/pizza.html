Pizza Pepperoni

The preparation of this pepperoni pizza may seem difficult, however, we have simplified the steps to prepare it in a very simple way, over time you will learn and you will be able to add any ingredient that you think makes it better and even prepare a pizza of a different flavor.

Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • 1 pizza mass
  • Mozzarella
  • Pepperoni
  • Grated
  • Cheese
  • Salami
  • Oregano

Method

First, on the mass, we spread a layer of tomato paste with the help of a knife. Then, we placed a layer of Mozzarella. Later, we cut 20 slices of pepperoni and uniformly distributed. We add a few pieces of salami. By last, we sprinkle grated cheese and oregano to our taste. We bake for 10 to 15 minutes at 180Â° and ready.
